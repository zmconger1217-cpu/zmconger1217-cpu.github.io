FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, complimentary worldwide shipping on all orders over $2,000. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items in original packaging. Bespoke and custom pieces are final sale.

Are your diamonds conflict-free?

Absolutely. All MINDJEWEL diamonds are sourced from suppliers certified under the Kimberley Process.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Each piece comes with a detailed care guide. We also offer complimentary cleaning and inspection at our atelier.
